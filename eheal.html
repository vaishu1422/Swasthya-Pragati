More about SWASTHYA PRAGATI:

SWASTHYA PRAGATI is an E-Healthcare website. It is a website that contains all information about a person's health problem, whether physical or mental and is a user-friendly, cost-effective, and secure website. The primary idea is to deliver all of the information that a person should know about their health by combining all health parameters .
Some features are:
  • It provides ayurvedic treatment as well as information on how to deal with mental stress, anxiety, depression ,and other health problems naturally with very less amount of treatment.

  • As mental health is a very sensitive topic these days and people don't take it seriously so this website would be the saviour for that. One can check the given checkboxes given with the symptoms and have a quick assessment and determine what issue they have and then they will get many suggestions to cure themselves.

  • It shows medicine and yoga suggestions according to your health status which is a self assessment.

  • Their is a daily activity tracker as well with the help of which patient can monitor himself/herself.

  • There is recommended reading material and videos about leading a healthy lifestyle as well as books and videos with information on various medical conditions.

  • There is distinct sections for different age groups, and treatment is advised accordingly.

  • A diet chart is provided based on age and health condition.

  • This website also serve as an online doctor consultation, medicine consultation, and local clinical reviews for in-person visits.
